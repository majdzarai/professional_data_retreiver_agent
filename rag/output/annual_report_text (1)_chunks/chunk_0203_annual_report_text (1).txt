Other Accelerators had a positive year of sales, with 18 machines sold, alongside agreements 
for upgrades, with an order intake of EUR 81 
million. Overall revenues grew more than 50%, with equipment revenues alone increasing 
58.2% to EUR 99.1 million. REBIT grew to EUR 
23.7 million, an increase of 293.7% year on year. This strong performance was underpinned by 
high value backlog conversion. 26 installations 
began in 2023, with 16 of them starting in the 
second half. 17 installations were completed in 
2023.
