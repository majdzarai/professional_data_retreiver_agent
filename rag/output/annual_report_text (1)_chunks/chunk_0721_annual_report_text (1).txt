Report 2023 
131. Accounting policy on revenue recognition 
IBA has the following revenue streams which will determine how the revenue is recognised. Equipment and installation services 
The main activity of the Group consists of the construction of proton-therapy and other accelerators equipment and the 
installation services for its customers. Such contracts with customers are referred to as equipment and installation services, it represents the most important portion of IBAs revenue.
