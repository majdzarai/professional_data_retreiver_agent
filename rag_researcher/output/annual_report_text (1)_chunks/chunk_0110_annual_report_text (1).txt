Protect, enhance and save lives 
by enabling INDEPENDENT 
QUALITY ASSURANCE
The priority of IBA in its dosimetry activity is to ensure that patients 
receive a safe, accurate and reliable diagnosis and treatment. In medical imaging and radiotherapy, radiation must be used 
with great caution and precision. The prescribed dose (expressed in Gray [Gy]) must be rigorously 
respected, both in terms of intensity and location. Patient lives, their safety and the success of their treatment depend upon it.
