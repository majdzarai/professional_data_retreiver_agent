We present to you our statutory auditors report in the context of our statutory audit of the consolidated 
accounts of Ion Beam Application SA (the Company) and its subsidiaries (jointly the Group). This 
report includes our report on the consolidated accounts, as well as the other legal and regulatory 
requirements. This forms part of an integrated whole and is indivisible. We have been appointed as statutory auditor by the general meeting dated 10 July 2023, following the 
proposal formulated by the board of directors and following the recommendation by the audit 
committee and the proposal formulated by the works council.
