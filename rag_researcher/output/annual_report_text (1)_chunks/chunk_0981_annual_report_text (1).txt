Accounting policy 
Classification and measurement 
Financial assets: The classification and measurement of the Groups financial assets are, as follows: Debt instruments at amortized cost: this category includes the Groups Trade receivables, long-term receivables on 
contracts in progress, other receivables and loans included under other long-term assets, non-trade receivables/advance 
payments, short-term receivables and cash and cash equivalents. Equity instruments at FVOCI, with no recycling of gains or losses to profit or loss on derecognition.
